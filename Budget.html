Budget Hotels in Colombo

Sri Lanka Foundation Institute

Today,the Sri Lanka Foundation is on a stronger footing whereby it is regularized under an act of Parliament,The Sri Lanka Foundation Law no.31 of 1973. Sri Lanka Foundation functions autonomously under the purview of HE The President of Sri Lanka and is Manage by a Board.

Learn More

Janaki Hotel Colombo

Hotel Janaki is located just 1 km from the Colombo Beach. It offers modern accommodation with an outdoor pool, 2 dining options and free Wi-Fi in public areas.Bandaranaike International Airport is a 50-minute drive from the hotel.Delicious Indian dishes can be enjoyed at the Lotus Restaurant. In-room dining is available upon request.

Learn More

Renuka City Hotel Colombo

Just 100 m from Liberty Plaza Shopping Complex, Renuka City Hotel has free parking and 3 restaurants and bars. A 24-hour front desk and round-the-clock room service is available.Bandaranaike International Airport is a 1-hour drive away.
